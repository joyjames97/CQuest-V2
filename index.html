Why choose CQuest?

We are here to to minimise risk and increase value for our clients

We are one of the leading cost consultancy in the Middle East, offering years of experience on some of the most ambitious, complex and schedule-accelerated projects in the world. We are fully registered and licensed to operate in all domains of operations across MENA by RICS and SAVE.

We have adopted BIM as a key tool for the integrated delivery of the Project through the enforcement and implementation of our BIM strategy for all participants.

Our extensive database of building cost information continually provides us with an almost unmatched knowledge base for projects of every description.
